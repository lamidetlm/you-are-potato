You are a potato

In life, be like a potato: stay rooted and keep growing.

A potato never judges another potato.

Even imperfect potatoes make delicious fries.

Life is like a potato: full of surprises beneath the surface.

Little potato poem:
Round and gentle under earth
Growing in mysterious mirth
Patient golden treasure found
Beloved bounty from the ground

A potato doesn't become mashed overnight.

Be proud to be a potato: you can become fries, mash, gratin...

Potato wisdom: the darker it gets, the more you grow.
